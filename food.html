Food in Edinburgh

The absolute top picks for true foodies looking to try fresh, authentic Scottish ingredients.

Aizle

Go to Aizle for an exquisite ever-changing menu of 6 courses per meal. They offer anything ranging from Koren kitchen to the classic beef steak. They also offer a detailed wine list, so you can enjoy a glass of fine white wine with your salmon. If you’re in the mood for a really fancy mean, head to Aizle!

107 St. Leonard’s St., Edinburgh, EH8 9QY
Contact: 0131 662 9349

Sonder

Scottish seasonal ingredients make up high-quality dishes with well matched flavours at Sonder. The Menu at Sonder is divided into garden, sea and land. With an open-plan dining and kitchen you can appreciate the food and the work behind it.

74 South Clerk St, Edinburgh, EH8 9PT
Contact: 0131 667 7032

The Table

Enjoy seven courses of outstanding food in the heart of Edinburgh’s cosmopolitan and cultural city centre. The food at the table focuses on European cooking with Scotland’s finest ingredients. With an open-floor kitchen, you will be able to be part of the cooking experience. You can see the food being prepared and chat with the chefs.

3A Dundas St., Edinburgh, EH3 6QG
Contact: 0131 281 1689

Number One at The Balmoral

Number One, Edinburgh's culinary landmark, is The Balmoral's Michelin-starred fine-dining restaurant. Executive Chef Jeff Bland places an emphasis on creative dishes whilst promoting the best of Scotland by using only the finest and freshest Scottish ingredients.

1 Princes St., Edinburgh, EH2 2EQ
Contact: 0131 557 6727

Music in Edinburgh

Here you can find a collection of the best live music in town. Whatever your music taste, you are guaranteed to have an amazing night out in any of these venues.

The Captain's Bar

And now...for the lovers of the unusual...we present: The Captain's bar. This venue’s got the best folk music in all of Edinburgh. If you’re in the mood for a cozy environment and some funky music with your pint, head to The Captain's Bar!

4 South College Street, Edinburgh, EH8 9AA
Contact: 0749 355 5702

The Voodoo Rooms

The Voodoo Rooms has live music nights for the lovers of jazz and an impeccable menu. The bar has won six awards including one for cocktails and one for their beautiful interior design. Get taken back to the Jazz Age in this Gatsby-mansion-like venue and feel the spirit of the flappers.

19a W Register St, Edinburgh EH2 2AA
Contact: 0131 556 7060

The Jazz Bar

Edinburgh’s Jazz Bar has three to five gigs everyday with musicians performing different types of genres including jazz, blues, funk, and soul. The Bar has won the Scotland’s Jazz Venue of the Year Award and is one of Edinburgh’s most prominent jazz bars. Go there if you want a relaxed evening filled with a pint and some music for the soul.

1 Chambers Street, Edinburgh EH1 1HR
Contact: 0131 220 4298

The Jam House

The Jam House has an open-concept floor plan allowing everyone to enjoy its live music from anywhere within the bar. It puts an emphasis on acoustics so the performers sound better than ever. This musical venue offers musics from every genre possible: swing, country, indie, pop, funk, and Latin. So if you don’t know what you want to do but you know you want to have an unforgettable night, choose The Jam House!

5 Queen St, Edinburgh, EH2 1JE
Contact: 0131 220 2321

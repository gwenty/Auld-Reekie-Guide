The Jazz Bar

Edinburgh’s Jazz Bar has three to five gigs everyday with musicians performing different types of genres including jazz, blues, funk, and soul. The Bar has won the Scotland’s Jazz Venue of the Year Award and is one of Edinburgh’s most prominent jazz bars. Go there if you want a relaxed evening filled with a pint and some music for the soul.

1 Chambers Street, Edinburgh EH1 1HR
Contact: 0131 220 4298

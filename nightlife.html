Nightlife in Edinburgh

Find the best night clubs in Edinburgh.

The Hive

Welcome to Edinburgh’s signature student nightclub - everyone’s been to Hive! Dive in a cool atmosphere as you navigate the several different rooms and vibes Hive offers. Dance away the night with all the chart hits you know and love! Get ready to be blown away by how cheap the alcohol is! Go to Hive for an unforgettable night!

15 Niddry St., Edinburgh, EH1 1LG
Contact: 0131 000 0000

Atik

Atik, also known as Cav to devoted locals, is one of the largest nightclubs in Edinburgh. It has a disco dance floor and the main ground floor, both offering mad tunes for days. A little more pricey than Hive, Atik still has a great atmosphere and every penny is worth it!

3 W Tollcross, Edinburgh, EH3 9BP
Contact: 0131 228 3252

Bourbon

Located in the most central part of Edinburgh, Bourbon is one way to class it up. With one of the best sound and light systems in town, Bourbon really lives up to the hype. Bourbon Milk Tuesdays are famous for getting wild. Come to Bourbon for a night of wild fun!

24 Frederick St, Edinburgh, EH2 2JR
Contact: 0131 322 3190

The Liquid Rooms

This club is something special for the lovers of rock’n’roll and alternative rock. Get away from the repetitive chart music night and get alternative instead! Friday nights are known as “Propaganda” and they are often themed. If you have high expectations but don’t know what to expect, go to The Liquid Rooms!

9C Victoria Street, Edinburgh, EH1 2HE
Contact: 0131 225 2564
